Configure Sound:

Set SYSTEM volume (not "normal" app volume)

1) Open the Settings app, click "Sounds & Haptics"
2) Move the "Ringer and Alerts" slider to the right so that it is loud
3) Make sure your phone is not on vibrate or "Do Not Disturb"


Disable "Hey Siri":

The "Hey Siri" feature is not permissible for use on Yom Tov since it engages an electronic device

1) Open the Settings app, click "Siri & Search"
2) Disable the switch: Listen for "Hey Siri"


Reschedule Buttons:

The reschedule buttons (① ② ③) allow you to quickly reschedule an alarm for 1, 2, or 3 days from the CURRENT date.

Simply tab the desired button to reschedule the alarm. It will automatically be enabled.


Reschedule Buttons:

To delete an alarm simply swipe the alarm left and tap "Delete"

Swiping left all the way to the edge of the screen will delete the alarm without the need to tap Delete
